ABOUT SEBASTE HOSPITAL

Sebaste Community Hospital is a trusted healthcare institution dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality medical services to the community. Committed to patient-centered care, the hospital offers comprehensive consultations, case management, laboratory partnerships, diagnostic tests, and maternal health services, ensuring that every individual receives the medical attention they need.


With a focus on maternal care, the hospital's obstetricians and gynecologists (OB-GYNs) provide essential prenatal, childbirth, and postpartum support for expectant mothers. In addition, vaccination programs, preventive healthcare services, and specialized treatments contribute to the hospital's goal of fostering a healthier and safer community.


Beyond medical treatment, Sebaste Community Hospital extends its commitment through free transportation assistance and health education programs, ensuring that healthcare remains accessible to all. Through compassionate service, expert medical care, and strong community partnerships, the hospital strives to uphold its mission of promoting wellness and improving lives.

Our history

Nestled along the vibrant shores of Antique, the municipality of Sebaste has long been a beacon of resilience and harmony. Founded in the early 18th century by settlers who believed in the power of nature and communal well-being, Sebaste thrived as a haven for health and sustainability. Originally a small fishing and farming village, the people of Sebaste embraced traditional healing practices, utilizing indigenous herbs and natural remedies passed down through generations. By the late 19th century, the municipality had become renowned for its holistic approach to wellness, drawing visitors seeking rejuvenation and tranquility.


With the turn of the 20th century, Sebaste further cemented its reputation as a model of community health, pioneering innovative local policies that ensured accessible healthcare and environmental preservation. The introduction of the "Healthy Community Pact" in 1995 united residents under a shared mission of wellness, leading to the establishment of sustainable farms, clean water systems, and community wellness centers. Today, Sebaste stands as a shining example of a municipality built on the foundations of health, unity, and sustainability—where every resident plays a vital role in nurturing the land and each other. Through generations, the spirit of wellness has remained at the heart of this thriving community.


Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional, compassionate healthcare while empowering our community through education, prevention, and accessible services—prioritizing maternal and child health, strengthening medical partnerships, and ensuring affordable treatment for all.

Vision

To be a leading healthcare provider in the community, ensuring that every individual receives holistic, patient-centered care, regardless of their background or financial status. We strive to promote health, wellness, and disease prevention through innovation, dedication, and compassion.
