OB-GYN SPECIALIST

At Sebaste Community Hospital our Obstetrics and Gynecology (OB-GYN) specialists provide compassionate care for women at every stage of life. We offer comprehensive services including prenatal care, childbirth, gynecological exams, family planning, and management of reproductive health conditions to support the well-being of women in our community.

Dr. Angela Torres, MD

OB-GYN

"Doktora Angela" specializes in women's health, prenatal care, and safe motherhood. She regularly holds check-ups for pregnant women, gives Tetanus Toxoid vaccines, and offers guidance on reproductive health. Local mothers lovingly call her "Nanay Doktora."
