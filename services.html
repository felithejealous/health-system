OUR SERVICES

Sebaste Community Hospital provides comprehensive healthcare services to meet the needs of its community. The hospital offers emergency care, outpatient consultations, maternal and child health services, and basic laboratory diagnostics. With a focus on accessible and compassionate care, it also provides health education and preventive medicine programs. Dedicated medical professionals ensure quality treatment for patients in a supportive environment.
