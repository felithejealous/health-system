OUR DOCTORS

At Sebaste Community Hospital, our compassionate healthcare team is wholeheartedly committed to safeguarding your well-being and providing exceptional medical care to every member of our community.

We take pride in delivering personalized, patient-centered services through our dedicated team of skilled physicians and specialists. Each doctor brings unique expertise, warm bedside manner, and deep commitment to serving the people of Sebaste. Whether you need routine check-ups, specialized diagnostics, maternal care, or dental services, our healthcare professionals are here to support your journey to better health with professionalism, empathy, and the highest standards of medical excellence.

Our doctors don't just treat conditions — they care for people. From the friendly, comprehensive care of our general practitioners to the specialized attention of our OB-GYN, radiologists, pathologists and dentists, every member of our medical staff is devoted to ensuring you receive the right treatment, the right way, with dignity and respect.

We understand that quality healthcare is built on trust, which is why our physicians take time to listen, explain, and develop treatment plans tailored to your unique needs. Their extensive training combined with genuine compassion makes Sebaste Community Hospital a place where patients feel valued and cared for at every visit.
