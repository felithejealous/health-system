FAQs

How can I book an appointment with a doctor?
  You can book an appointment by calling our hospital at the provided contact numbers or visiting the front desk during working hours.
Are walk-in consultations allowed?
  Yes, walk-in consultations are allowed depending on the availability of the doctor.
What should I bring to my appointment?
  Please bring a valid ID, any relevant medical records, and your health insurance card (if applicable).
How do I know which doctor is right for me?
  You may call us or visit our website's "Find Doctors" section to view profiles and specializations.
Do you accept emergency walk-ins?
  Yes, our emergency department is open 24/7 to accommodate emergency walk-ins.
